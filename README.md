# Sakura Elite - Resident Portal

A simple web portal for Sakura Elite apartment residents to view bills, make payments, and manage their unit information.

## What This Does

### For Residents:
- View your unit info and payment history
- Pay rent, water bills, and maintenance online (via Razorpay)
- Update tenant names and vehicle counts
- Filter and search through past payments

### For Admin:
- Manage all units from one dashboard
- Generate monthly billing automatically
- Track water tankers and calculate water bills
- Monitor maintenance expenses and fund balances
- Mark payments as received

## Login Details

**Resident Access:**
- Units: GF1, GF2, FF1, FF2, SF1, SF2, TF1, TF2, T2
- Default PIN: `1234` (change these in production!)

**Admin Access:**
- PIN: `1470`

## How to Use

### For Residents
1. Select your unit and enter your PIN
2. View your dashboard with unit details
3. Click "Pay Now" on any pending bill to pay online
4. Use the navigation to browse payment history
5. Edit tenant names or vehicle counts as needed

### For Admin
1. Login with admin PIN (1470)
2. Click "Auto-Generate This Month" to create bills for all units
3. Go to "Water" to add tanker deliveries and calculate water bills
4. Go to "Maintenance" to track expenses and fund balances
5. Edit any billing amounts directly in the table

## Important Notes

- The app works on mobile phones, tablets, and computers
- All payment data is stored securely
- Water bills are calculated automatically based on meter readings
- Maintenance and corpus funds are tracked separately
